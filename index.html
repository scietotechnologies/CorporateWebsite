Scieto Vision

Scieto Technologies was founded as woman-owned small business focused on delivering agricultural and biotechnology solutions to serve the growing needs of US farmers. Scieto Technologies was founded in 2018 and operates in Loudoun County, Virginia. Loudoun County is situated in the Dulles Technology Corridor and has historical links to the agricultural sector.

We are a group of scientists and bioengineers who are developing remote monitoring systems capable of continuously and non-invasively managing the health of crops and farm animals.
